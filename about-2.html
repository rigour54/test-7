About Us

We are your one-stop for government grants and funding.

We work with Ontario-based businesses to achieve their goals and help fund their future.
Grant consulting, writing, management and reporting services, long term funding strategies, digital adoption consultation and so much more!We will match your organization with the right grants to maximize your chances for success.

About Founder

Kirsten Scott is a registered Lobbyist with the Office of the Commissioner of Lobbying of Canada.She has held academic positions, managed research and development projects in the both the Nuclear and Water Treatment sectors, and has served as a consultant for product development, business accreditation, training and QAC/QC program development, and digital adoption strategies before starting Springboard Grants and Consulting. Kirsten has helped many companies achieve their goals over the years.

Kirsten has helped many companies receive financial assistance through the CanExport SME program, IRAP, IDEA Fund, ThriveForward Fund, CM&E Technology Fund, the Eastern Ontario Development Fund (EODF), the Canadian Agricultural Partnership (CAP), the Canadian Ontario Job Grant (COJG), SR&ED claims and many others.
